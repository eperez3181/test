Summary: Covid-19 Analysis
(Confirmed Cases and Death)

The purpose of our COVID-19 analysis was to illustrate the case and death rate across global regions as well as to identify the largest countries with cases, infections, and death rate.


Using credible sources such as the World Health Organization (WHO), the U.S. Census Bureau and the New York Times, we were able to scrape infection and death rate data from over 200 countries and compile them into PGAdmin database.
