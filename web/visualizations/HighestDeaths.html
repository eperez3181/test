Highest Covid Deaths

This graph can be compared alongside the infection graph to help illustrate the relationship between infection and death. The United States experienced the sharpest spike in death rate at the start of our analyzed period which coincides with the sharp increase of infection rate at the beginning. The other four countries did not have this sharp increase but rather saw a steady death rate throughout.
