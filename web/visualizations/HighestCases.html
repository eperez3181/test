Highest Covid Cases

Through our data collection we were able to track the 5 highest infected countries with the United States, Brazil, India, Germany, and France taking the top 5 spots. The United States with its large population experienced the highest number of infections during our analyzed period with a sharp increase at the beginning which slowed into a still-increasing infection. India and France had similar trajectories. Germany experienced slower but more sustained growth throughout.
